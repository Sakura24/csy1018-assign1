Mijala Shakya was born and raised in Kathmandu,Nepal since 1997. She is currently a first year student studying Bsc.(Hons)Computing in NAMI(Naya Aayam Multidiscilinary Institute). She had passed her SLC(School Leaving Certificate) Examination in 2014 from GEMS(Graded English Medium School). She had recently passed her A-level Examination in 2016 from Trinity Internation College.

She has been an active member of Rotraract Club of Bagmati,Nepal since 2016. As a member she has participated in many programmes such as Cycling for a cause on Cancer Day. She has also participated in the proposal writing training held by the Rotary club of Dillibazar. She has even participated in RYLA(Rotary Youth Leadership Award) as a rotracter. She is even an active volunteer. She had volunteered in many programmes such as the tree plantation programme, blood donation, stationary distribution and eye check-up program hosted by Rotary club of Bagmati.

Aside from this she is a great cook and an animal lover. In her spare time she likes to study novels and cook. She loves to collect wood craft items . She is also good with origami.
